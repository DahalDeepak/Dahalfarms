Welcome to Dahal Agriculture and Livestock Farm

Welcome to Dahal Agriculture and Livestock Farm

We are dedicated to providing the best agricultural and livestock products in the region. Our farm is committed to sustainable and organic farming practices to ensure the highest quality produce and livestock for our customers.

At Dahal Agriculture and Livestock Farm, we believe in the power of nature and the importance of preserving our environment. Our farm is a testament to our dedication to eco-friendly practices and our passion for agriculture.

About Us

Dahal Agriculture and Livestock Farm is a family-owned business situated in Gaindakot, Nawalparasi-East, Nepal. We specialize in organic farming and livestock rearing. Our farm has been in operation for over two decades, providing fresh and healthy products to our community.

Our mission is to promote sustainable agriculture and support the local economy. We take pride in our ethical farming practices and our commitment to animal welfare. Our team of experienced farmers and livestock handlers work tirelessly to ensure that our products meet the highest standards of quality and safety.

We invite you to visit our farm and experience the beauty of nature and the bounty of our harvest. Join us in our journey towards a healthier and more sustainable future.
